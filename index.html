About Me

My name is Edric, and I am a student at The Coding Boot Camp at UCLA Extention. I enjoy PC gaming, and love to geek out about computer hardware. I have built multiple gaming desktops over the years - overclocking CPU's to their limit and outfitting them with top of the line cooling systems - and through this I have gotten somewhat computer hardware. Also through my love of gaming, I have joined the modding community and have done some pseudo-coding, which was simply changing some scripts in the game logs. I love being on computers and am very passionate about them, and I realized that I needed work in a field that I love and enjoy. So I took the step towards becoming a Software Developer by enrolling in Coding Boot Camp. I am very excited to be a student at The Coding Bootcamp and am loving to get to know how software works.
